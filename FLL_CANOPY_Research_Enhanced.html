✨ Quick Tips for Success

🎯

Start Small

Don't try to save the entire planet! Help ONE farmer, protect ONE type of bee, clean ONE pond. Small wins lead to big impacts.

🗣️

Talk to Humans

Real users > assumptions. Interview farmers, rangers, scientists BEFORE building anything. Understand their real problems!

🔧

Simple > Complex

Broken robots score zero points. Simple, reliable solutions win every time. Test often, improve continuously!

📈

Measure Impact

How do you know it works? Track time saved, ecosystems helped, or people reached. Numbers tell your story!

🤝

Teamwork Wins

Divide tasks fairly. Everyone contributes. Celebrate small wins together. Strong teams build strong innovations!

🎨

Be Creative

Nature inspires amazing solutions. Think like a bee, leaf, or termite! Copy nature's best ideas and make them your own.
